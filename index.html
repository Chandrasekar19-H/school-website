The PSBB Millennium School in Chennai is frequently ranked among the city’s top schools because it offers a blend of strong academics, holistic development, and modern teaching methods. It follows the CBSE curriculum from primary classes through higher secondary, which means students study the nationwide standard syllabus and appear for CBSE board exams in Classes X and XII. This affiliation ensures that the school follows recognized academic standards and assessment methods set by CBSE, making it a credible choice for quality education in Chennai.
